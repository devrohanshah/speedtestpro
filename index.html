About SpeedTest

Welcome to SpeedTest, your reliable tool for measuring internet connection performance. Launched in 2023, SpeedTest aims to provide users with accurate, easy-to-understand metrics about their internet speed.

Our platform measures four key aspects of your connection:

  • Download Speed - How quickly data is transferred from the internet to your device
  • Upload Speed - How quickly data is transferred from your device to the internet
  • Ping - The response time between your device and the server (lower is better)
  • Jitter - The variation in ping over time, indicating connection stability

Using SpeedTest is simple - just click the START button and wait for the test to complete. Our algorithm selects the best server based on your location for the most accurate results, but you can also manually select from our network of servers worldwide.

SpeedTest was created to help users troubleshoot connection issues, verify if they're getting the internet speeds promised by their service provider, and make informed decisions about their internet needs.

Whether you're a casual internet user, a remote worker, or an avid gamer, having insight into your connection performance can help you optimize your online experience.

Feel free to contact us with any questions, suggestions, or feedback through our Contact page.
